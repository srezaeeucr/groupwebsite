Galaxy Formation & Evolution

Our research focuses on understanding how galaxies form and evolve over cosmic time through detailed spectroscopic analysis and innovative observational techniques.

Spectroscopic Properties

Analyzing emission lines, stellar populations, and chemical abundances to trace galaxy evolution.

Subkpc Scale Studies

High-resolution observations revealing the internal structure and dynamics of galaxies.

Pixel SED Fitting

Spatially resolved analysis of stellar populations and star formation histories.
